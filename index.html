The science

Built on clinical
research.

Every feature in NeuroClear is grounded in peer-reviewed research on dementia detection and cognitive intervention.

Speech analysis

Speech patterns as early biomarkers

Research shows changes in speech — increased pauses, word-finding difficulty, and reduced fluency — appear years before a formal dementia diagnosis is possible.

Cognitive testing

Standardized bedside assessment

NeuroClear incorporates the 3-word recall test, clock drawing test, and orientation assessment — all validated tools used in standard clinical dementia screening.

Neuroplasticity

Brain training & cognitive reserve

Cognitive stimulation therapy has been shown in multiple clinical trials to improve quality of life and slow decline in people with mild-to-moderate dementia.

Dataset

Trained on real speech data

Our risk model was developed using a Kaggle dataset of over 1,000 real patient speech recordings, each analyzed for acoustic and linguistic dementia markers.
